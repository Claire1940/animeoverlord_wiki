## FAQ

**Q: Who is the actual creator of the Overlord anime?**

A: The original story was created by Kugane Maruyama (the author). The anime is produced by Studio Madhouse and directed by Naoyuki Itō. When people look for the **overlord anime creator other animes**, they are usually looking for other works by these three entities.

**Q: Has Kugane Maruyama written any other full-length series?**

A: Currently, Maruyama is primarily focused on finishing Overlord. He has written several short stories and participated in crossover projects like *Isekai Quartet*, but he has not started a new long-form light novel series yet.

**Q: Why is Studio Madhouse so famous among Overlord fans?**

A: Madhouse is known for its ability to adapt "unadaptable" stories with high-quality art and faithful pacing. Their work on *One Punch Man* and *Hunter x Hunter* proved they could handle the massive power scales and complex characters found in Overlord.

**Q: Are there any other animes that feel like Overlord but aren't made by the same team?**

A: Yes, *The Saga of Tanya the Evil* and *Hellsing Ultimate* are often recommended. While they have different creators, they share the same dark atmosphere, powerful protagonists, and mature themes that Overlord fans enjoy.
