## The 41 Supreme Beings: Creators of the Legend

The "Supreme Beings" were the human players who formed the guild Ainz Ooal Gown. While most logged out before the server shutdown, their legacy lives on through the NPCs they meticulously crafted. These creators are revered as deities by the denizens of Nazarick.

### Ainz Ooal Gown (Momonga)
The protagonist, formerly known as Suzuki Satoru, is the Guildmaster and the only player to remain with the NPCs during the transition. As an Overlord-class undead, he possesses an arsenal of 718 spells—far exceeding the standard limit of 300 for level 100 players. He is a master tactician who operates under extreme caution, often using his "Momon" persona to gather intelligence as an Adamantite-ranked adventurer.

### Touch Me
A founder of the original clan "Nine's Own Goal," Touch Me was one of only nine players to hold the "World Champion" class. He was a paragon of justice and the creator of Sebas Tian. His armor, "Compliance with Law," surpassed Divine-class items, making him one of the few individuals Ainz could never defeat in a duel.

### Peroroncino and Bukubukuchagama
This sibling duo was responsible for some of the most iconic NPCs. Peroroncino, a hardcore power gamer with a love for "H-games," created Shalltear Bloodfallen, infusing her with his various fetishes. His sister, Bukubukuchagama, was a famous voice actress who created the twins Aura and Mare. As a "Slime Shield" tank, she was known as "The Unsinkable" for her legendary defensive capabilities.

## The Floor Guardians: The Shield of Nazarick

The Floor Guardians are the highest-ranking NPCs within the Great Tomb. Each is responsible for defending specific levels and possesses power that dwarfs almost any inhabitant of the New World.

### Albedo: Guardian Overseer
As the highest-ranked NPC, Albedo supervises the other guardians and manages the internal affairs of Nazarick. She is a Succubus with the highest defensive stats in the tomb, capable of redirecting damage to her legendary armor, Hermes Trismegistus. Due to a last-minute setting change by Ainz, she is obsessively in love with him, which often leads to a fierce rivalry with Shalltear.

### Shalltear Bloodfallen (Floors 1-3)
Shalltear is a True Vampire and the most powerful Floor Guardian in a one-on-one fight (excluding the strategic golem Gargantua). She is a divine magic caster whose build is a perfect counter to other magic users and necromancers. Her signature weapon, the Spruit Lance, allows her to recover health with every successful strike.

### Cocytus (Floor 5)
A massive four-armed insectoid warrior, Cocytus embodies the code of bushido. He is a weapon master capable of wielding 21 different armaments simultaneously. Following his experiences with the Lizardmen, he has grown from a simple soldier into a nuanced leader who understands the value of mercy and strategic loyalty.

### Aura and Mare (Floor 6)
The Dark Elf twins represent two sides of the same coin. Aura is a Beast Tamer and Ranger who specializes in group combat and guerrilla tactics, utilizing a vast collection of high-level magical beasts. Mare, despite his timid appearance, is one of the strongest magic casters in Nazarick, specializing in Druidic magic and massive area-of-effect spells like "Earthquake."

> **💡 Pro Tip:** While Shalltear is the strongest in 1v1 combat, Mare possesses the highest destructive potential for city-scale demolition, as seen during the fall of the Re-Estize Kingdom.

## Strategic Genius and Espionage: Demiurge

Demiurge, the Guardian of the 7th Floor, is the intellectual engine behind Nazarick’s expansion. Operating under the alias "Jaldabaoth," he orchestrates complex political maneuvers to destabilize human nations. His "Abellian Sheep" project and the management of the Great Forest of Tob showcase his ruthless efficiency. 

Demiurge’s combat style is unique; he uses "Aspects of the Devil" to transform his body parts into weapons. While his raw stats are lower than Cocytus or Shalltear, his ability to summon high-level demons and his "Command Mantra," which forces the weak-willed to obey his voice, make him a terrifying opponent for anyone in the New World.

## The Specialized Guardians: Gargantua and Victim

Not all **overlord anime characters** were custom-made by the guild members. Gargantua, the 4th Floor Guardian, is a mindless siege golem awarded to the guild by the game system. It possesses stats that exceed even Shalltear, but it requires direct manual activation by Ainz to function.

Victim, the 8th Floor Guardian, is perhaps the most unique. Appearing as a pink fetus, Victim is intentionally weak. Its primary purpose is to be killed; upon death, it triggers a massive, inescapable debuff that traps all enemies in the vicinity. This "tactical sacrifice" was instrumental in defeating a 1,500-player invasion during the guild's peak years in YGGDRASIL.

## Combat Power and Leveling in 2026

In the context of the New World, the power scaling of **overlord anime characters** is almost absolute. Most Floor Guardians are level 100, whereas the strongest "Hero" class humans rarely exceed level 30.

1. **Level 100 NPCs**: These include all Floor Guardians (except Victim) and Sebas Tian. They possess Divine-class equipment and World Items for protection.
2. **The Pleiades**: The Battle Maids of Nazarick, ranging from level 46 to 63. They serve as the final line of defense before the Throne Room.
3. **Area Guardians**: Powerful entities like Pandora's Actor (Ainz's personal creation) who manage specific locations within floors.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Never underestimate a character based on their appearance. Mare Bello Fiore’s feminine attire and shy demeanor mask a "Petite Catastrophe" spell capable of wiping out entire armies in seconds.

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the strongest among the overlord anime characters?**

A: In terms of raw 1v1 combat ability, Shalltear Bloodfallen is considered the strongest. However, in terms of pure stats and siege potential, the golem Gargantua holds the top spot. Ainz Ooal Gown is the most versatile due to his massive spell list and tactical brilliance.

**Q: Why does Albedo hate the other Supreme Beings?**

A: While she is fanatically loyal to Ainz, Albedo harbors a secret resentment toward the other 40 Supreme Beings for "abandoning" the guild and leaving Ainz alone. This has led her to form a secret unit with the goal of hunting down any other players who might appear in the New World.

**Q: Are Aura and Mare boys or girls?**

A: Aura Bella Fiora is a girl who dresses in masculine clothing, while Mare Bello Fiore is a boy who wears feminine attire. Their creator, Bukubukuchagama, designed them this way as a "crossdressing" gag and to reflect her belief that younger brothers should be submissive to their older sisters.

**Q: What is the purpose of Pandora's Actor?**

A: Pandora's Actor is an Area Guardian of the Treasury and Ainz's personal creation. He is a Doppelganger who can transform into any of the 41 Supreme Beings, copying 80% of their power. He serves as Ainz's body double and the manager of the guild's financial resources.
