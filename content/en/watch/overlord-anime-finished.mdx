## Ainz Ooal Gown: The Adaptable Overlord

Ainz (formerly Momonga) may not have the raw stats of Touch Me or the burst potential of Ulbert, but his survival is rooted in his tactical genius. As the Guild Master, he utilized **Dark Wisdom** to learn over 700 spells—more than double the limit of a standard level 100 player. Now that the **overlord anime finished** its primary run, his mastery over the New World is undisputed not just because of his magic, but because of his preparation.

1.  **Super Tier Magic:** Spells like *Fallen Down* and *Ia Shub-Niggurath* allow him to erase entire armies.
2.  **Strategic Gear Swapping:** Ainz possesses the equipment of his former guildmates, allowing him to adapt to any opponent's weakness.
3.  **Perfect Warrior:** A transformation spell that converts his magic levels into warrior levels, making him a deadly melee combatant.

## The Unstoppable NPCs: Rubedo and Albedo

Within the hierarchy of Nazarick, the NPCs created by the Supreme Beings often surpass the players themselves in specific roles. 

### Rubedo: The Strongest Entity
Rubedo is the younger sister of Albedo and is confirmed to be the strongest individual in Nazarick. Her creation involved a unique, mysterious process—implied to involve a World Class Item or an exploit. She is so powerful that even Ainz believes he would need to arm the entities of the 8th Floor with World Items just to stand a chance against her.

### Albedo: The Ultimate Defense
As the Guardian Overseer, Albedo is the pinnacle of tanking. Her armor, **Hermes Trismegistus**, consists of three layers that can absorb massive amounts of damage. Her unique skill allows her to redirect any incoming damage—even from Super Tier spells—directly into her armor, effectively making her body invulnerable until the armor itself shatters.

## S-Tier Floor Guardians: The Pillars of Nazarick

The Floor Guardians are the bosses of their respective domains, each optimized for a specific type of warfare. With the **overlord anime finished** showcasing their growth, we can see how they have adapted to the New World's unique challenges.

### Shalltear Bloodfallen
Shalltear is the best one-on-one fighter among the guardians. Her **Spruit Lance** allows her to recover health based on the damage she deals, making her nearly impossible to kill in a duel. Her "Blood Frenzy" state increases her power significantly, though it causes a temporary loss of self-control.

### Mare Bello Fiore
Despite his timid appearance, Mare is the most destructive Floor Guardian in terms of Area of Effect (AoE) damage. His druid magic allows him to manipulate the very earth, casting spells like *Earthquake* that can level entire cities in seconds. In 2026 retrospectives, Mare is often cited as the MVP of the Re-Estize Kingdom campaign.

### Cocytus
The four-armed weapon master of the 5th floor, Cocytus, possesses an encyclopedic knowledge of weapons. He can wield up to 21 different armaments, including the **God Slaying Emperor Blade**. His exoskeleton provides natural armor that regenerates alongside his health, making him a persistent threat in melee combat.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Never underestimate Cocytus's honor. While he is a brutal warrior, he often spares those he deems "true warriors," which can sometimes conflict with Nazarick's absolute directives.

## The New World Threats: True Dragon Lords

While Nazarick is dominant, they are not alone at the top. The True Dragon Lords are the original rulers of the New World, utilizing **Wild Magic**—a system that predates the Tier Magic brought by players.

*   **Platinum Dragon Lord (Tsaindorcus Vaision):** The son of the Dragon Emperor, he guards the guild base of the Eight Greed Kings. His Wild Magic can create a **World Isolating Barrier** that cuts off all teleportation and surveillance.
*   **Deep Darkness Dragon Lord:** This reclusive dragon once defeated a player 300 years ago and possesses one of the "Twenty" World Items. He is said to be able to defeat any Floor Guardian in a 1v1, with the exception of the massive construct Gargantua.

## Specialized Guardians: Pandora's Actor and Aureole Omega

Finally, we have the "Wild Cards" of Nazarick. These individuals are rarely seen on the front lines but are essential to the tomb's survival.

**Pandora's Actor** is a high-tier Doppelganger who can mimic any of the 41 Supreme Beings at 80% of their original strength. This flexibility allows him to fill any role—from a frontline tank to a backline healer—at a moment's notice.

**Aureole Omega** is the only human NPC in Nazarick and the leader of the Pleiades. She is a level 100 Commander who provides massive buffs to her allies. She also controls the teleportation gates of the tomb, ensuring that no intruder can navigate Nazarick without her permission.
